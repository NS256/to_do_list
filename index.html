Adding a new task:

Too add a new item to your to do list type it into the text box and press enter on your keyboard.

Editing an existing task:

If you make a mistake you can add edit an item by clicking on the edit icon.

Remove a task from the list:

You can remove items from the list by clicking the delete button.

List cleared after 30 days:

Please note, all lists are cleared after 30 days. When you make a change to your list this timer will be reset back to 30 days.
You can also reset the timer by clicking here.

Using Different Web Browsers

This tool uses cookies to save the items in your to do list. Some web browsers control how cookies are stored and accessed, if you experience issues with your to do list not being saved to you may need to change your cookie settings.
